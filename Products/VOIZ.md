# VOIZ

## Product Overview
VOIZ is Predixion AI's conversational voice AI platform for inbound and outbound customer communication. It automates high-volume voice interactions across collections, customer service, and sales workflows in BFSI and enterprise environments.

## Core Capabilities
- Inbound call automation — handles FAQs, account queries, complaint routing
- Outbound dialling — automated follow-up calls for collections and renewals
- Multilingual support — Hindi, English, and regional language variants
- Real-time escalation — routes complex queries to human agents with full context handoff
- System prompt versioning — deployments managed via structured prompt libraries
