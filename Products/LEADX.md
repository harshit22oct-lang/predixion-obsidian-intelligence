# LEADX

## Product Overview
LEADX is Predixion AI's AI lead qualification and outreach platform for B2B and B2C sales pipelines. It automates top-of-funnel lead engagement, qualification, and handoff to human sales reps — compressing the time from lead capture to qualified meeting.

## Core Capabilities
- Automated multi-channel outreach (email, WhatsApp, voice)
- AI-driven lead scoring and qualification based on custom criteria
- CRM integration (HubSpot, Salesforce) for seamless handoff
- Conversation intelligence — records and analyses every lead interaction
- Configurable qualification scripts per segment or product

## Target Use Cases
- BFSI: Loan lead qualification, cross-sell outreach
- EdTech: Admissions funnel automation
- SaaS: Inbound trial-to-demo conversion

## Current Status
- Product in active BD pipeline — not yet in enterprise deployment
- Pilot discussions ongoing with prospects in edtech and BFSI verticals

## Integration Notes
- REST API for CRM sync
- Webhook-based lead intake (from website forms, ad platforms)
- Requires 2-week onboarding for custom qualification script setup
